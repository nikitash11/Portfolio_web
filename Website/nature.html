PORTFOLIO:- NATURE

Gardens, landscapes, flowers, waterfalls, and trees surround us; nature is a magnificent thing. Whether it is a flower bed in your back yard, or an overgrown waterfall in a local creek, or the landscape view at the highest vantage point of the highway, the beauty of nature is unmistakable. For many, one way to enjoy nature is through photography or videography with either a digital camera, nature camera, or even a security camera. Nature photography is a very fulfilling and exciting hobby; it can seem overwhelming at first for an amateur who possibly does not understand the equipment, or is unaware about how to catch the true essence of the flowers or trees, but with a few solid guidelines any beginning can become a seasoned pro in no time!
